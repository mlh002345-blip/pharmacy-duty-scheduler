DejaVu Sans (Regular and Bold) — bundled for PDF export so Turkish
characters (ş, ğ, ı, İ, ö, ü, ç) render correctly without depending on
whatever fonts happen to be installed on the host.

DejaVu fonts are based on the Bitstream Vera fonts and are released
under a permissive license that explicitly allows embedding,
modification, and redistribution. Full license text:
https://dejavu-fonts.github.io/License.html
